Micro Loans

Description: Many times, donations that are sent for crisis relief help but only temporarily. Even if the need is met, families often remain in poverty. Micro loans are designed to establish ongoing sustainability that goes beyond relief & provides a sustainable source of income for families. In order for families to receive a micro loan to start a small business, there is a process of training & accountability that includes...

⚬ An interview and application

⚬ Assessment of references and reputation by a community coordinator

⚬ Training and classes for skill development

⚬ Mentoring, coaching, and ongoing accountability

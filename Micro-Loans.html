Micro Loans
& Small Business Development

Description: Many times, donations that are sent for crisis relief help but only temporarily. Even if the need it met, families often remain in poverty. Micro loans are designed to establish ongoing sustainability that goes beyond relief & provides a sustainable source of income for families. Our focus is giving families who lack education, skills, and resources the opportunity to obtain the means to start an income generating business. These are simple businesses that require small initial investments and will not only provide for the family but will benefit the entire community as well.

Strategy: In order for families to receive a micro loan to start a small business, there is a process of training & accountability. The process includes...

⚬ An interview and application

⚬ Assessment of references & reputation by community coordinator

⚬ Training & classes for skill development

⚬ Mentoring, coaching, & ongoing accountability

LEVELS: There are levels for a family to receive a micro loan. Everyone starts at level 1 which includes a small seed investment of $300 or less. If they show evidence of handling the loan responsibly, paying the full loan amount back, and successfully stewarding the business, they can apply for a larger micro loan by interviewing for a level 2 micro investment.

A family can follow this process and continue to climb the micro loan investment levels to keep to growing, employing, & expanding their business. Here are the micro loan levels...

⚬ Level 1: $300 or less

⚬ Level 2: $600 or less

⚬ Level 3: $900 or less

⚬ Level 4: $1,200 or less

⚬ Level 5: $1,500 or less

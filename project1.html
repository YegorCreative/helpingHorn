Community Recreation Area
& Soccer fields

Description: Due to the lack of recreational facilities, many kids in IDP camps end up getting pulled into drugs and other activities. Right now there is no place other than a rock area for kids to play soccer & stay active. Community leaders shared their heart and vision for the kids to have a place to play soccer on turf fields and stay out of trouble. We will help coach community leaders how to utilize the field for bringing the community together developing coaches, clinics, teams, & leagues. Our vision is to build recreation areas & soccer fields in IDP camps and villages that have none.

COST: $10,000 (per field)

1 field = $10k
